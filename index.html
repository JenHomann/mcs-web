    • Our Mission: To champion gender equity in tech through beginner and intermediate coding education for women, femmes, and non-men

    Mystery Code Society

    Tech and The Case of the
    Missing Genders

    Mystery Code Society offers free, beginner to intermediate coding education to women, femmes, and non-men. Educational offerings include classes, workshops, meetups, and other educational events. Our focus is on students in 5th-12th grades but may include programs for women, femmes, and non-men of all ages.

    Our board members are Rachel Fox, Wendy Holley, Shannon Jackson, Eris Koleszar, Stefanie Monge, Laurel Oetken, Jessica Selde, and Morgan Smith.
