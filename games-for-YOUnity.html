Games for YOUnity

Applications Open Now

In this course, students will learn:

  • Video Game Development
  • Unity Game Engine
  • C# programming language

Games For YOUnity is a free, intermediate code class for 7th-12th grade girls, femmes, and non-men*. Students should have prior experience in coding but may be new to Unity and/or C#. A home computer is not required to take this class. This class is a prerequisite for our summer class in which students design and build their own video game.

Eris Koleszar will be the instructor for this class. The session will run for 10 weeks, on Saturdays from 9:30 am to 12:30 pm from March 10th - May 19th, (no class scheduled on March 31st) at the Abrahams Branch Library.

Applications are due by Thursday, March 8th. Class size is limited to 12 students.

*This includes students who were not assigned female at birth and those who identify as femme, gender fluid, or gender non-conforming.
