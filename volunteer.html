    Board of Directors

    Our board members are nominated by committee and elected biennially. We have 4-10 board members including four officer positions. All Directors and members of the Advisory Council, Nominating Committee, and Grievance/Code of Conduct Committees must be approved by a majority vote of the active Directors.
